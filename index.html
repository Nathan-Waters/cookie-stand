About Us

We here at Salmon cookies world wide have one goal in mind and thats getting fish in your mouth. We have weeks of experience in the art and craft of fish shaped bakery goods. We are based out of Seattle AKA the salmon cookie kings of the north west based on an article in the Othello WA magazine.

We are proud sponsors of the Evergeen toddler soccer league and the University of Washington junior varsity beer pong league. As a team we have decided that giving back to the community is our main priority.
